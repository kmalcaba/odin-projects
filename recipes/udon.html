Chicken Udon Noodle Soup Recipe

Description

Udon is a type of ramen which has thick noodles instead of the standard thin noodles. Enjoy!

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 15 mins
  • Total: 35 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ (32 fluid ounce) containers chicken stock
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 large cooked skinless, boneless chicken
  • 1 head bok choy, chopped
  • ¼ cup dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 (7 ounce) packages dried udon noodles
  • ½ cup mung bean sprouts
  • 1 green onion, sliced diagonally
  • 2 tablespoons dried minced onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

Directions

  1. Bring chicken stock, garlic, ginger, and chili powder to a boil in a pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken, bok choy, and mushrooms. Let simmer lightly for 3 minutes.
  3. Add noodles and cook soup for 4 minutes more.
  4. Pour soup mixture evenly into 2 bowls. Place mung beans on top in the center of each bowl, with green onion placed neatly on top.
  5. Garnish with dried onions and cilantro.
